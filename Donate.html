Donation

Please consider donating. With your financial support we can continue to clean our forests. If you want to contribute regularly, you can do that by checking "Donate regularly" and then choosing between the given options. All common payment methods are available. If you want something back, you might be interested in our new merchandise shop.

You only have a few bucks in your pocket and think that wouldn't help? We want you to know, that even the smallest donation can make a difference. And if you don't have the financial means you can even help without money. Help spread the word or consider viewing our "Take Action" Page and learn what you can do to make our forest clean again.
